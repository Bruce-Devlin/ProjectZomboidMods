description=Getting Old – Dynamic Aging System
description=Ever wondered what surviving the apocalypse for years would really do to your body?
description=Getting Old adds a dynamic, immersive aging system to Project Zomboid. Your character now has a real age, a birthday, and gradually changes over time – for better or worse.
description=Youth comes with advantages. Old age comes with consequences.
description=
description=Features:
description=- Real Age System
description=- Characters start with an actual age based on selected traits
description=- Randomized birthday (day and month. year is based on trait or also random)
description=- Age increases naturally as in-game years pass
description=- Physical stat Changes Over Time (based on the four groups above)
description=
description=Your age affects:
description=- Endurance
description=- Movement speed
description=- Pain sensitivity
description=- Depression
description=- Fatigue
description=- Chance to stumble or fall (elderly)
description=
description=Trait Integration:
description=Age groups are implemented as real traits:
description=
description=Trait ----- Effect
description=Young ----- Extra endurance | Higher pain tolerance | Less stressed
description=Adult ----- Balanced/Base stats but at least you're not 45
description=Middle-aged ----- Less endurance | Lower pain tolerance | More stressed
description=Elderly ----- No endurance | No pain tolerance | Always stressed | Might fall over
description=
description=Young and Adult are positive traits.
description=Middle-aged and Elderly are negative traits.
description=
description=Immersive Details:
description=Stores birthday and age in player mod data
description=Designed to be compatible with other mods
description=
description=How It Works:
description=On first spawn, your age is assigned automatically
description=A birthday is generated
description=Each year your age increases
description=Stat effects update dynamically
description=Elderly survivors may occasionally stumble while moving
description=No configuration required — just subscribe and play.
description=
description=Compatibility:
description=Safe to add to existing saves (new characters is recommended otherwise you may be at RN-Jesus's mercy)
description=Works in singleplayer and multiplayer
description=Designed to avoid overwriting vanilla systems
description=Lightweight and performance friendly
description=
description=For fellow Modders:
description=Includes a simple shared DevTools.lua helper library that I'm building out for my mods.
description=
description=Stat-based design
description=Uses Stats API instead of direct player mutation
description=
description=Modular age system
description=Easy to extend (diseases, vision loss, arthritis, etc.)
description=
description=Notes:
description=Elderly characters are playable, but challenging
description=This mod does not change zombie behaviour
